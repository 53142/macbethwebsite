Politics

Political & Historical Context

Queen Elizabeth I of England died without an heir, so her cousin, King James I, inherited the English throne. King James I ruled both England and Scotland because of this. During this period, there were concerns about legitimacy surrounding the succession of the English crown. Likely partially fueled by this, there were multiple assassination attempts against King James I as well.

Cultural & Societal Beliefs

King James I strongly believed in witchcraft, causing him to persecute many women as witches.

Macbeth Connection

Macbeth implements the ideas of the divine right of kings from this period of time, demonstrating how illegitimate rulers can lead to disastrous consequences
